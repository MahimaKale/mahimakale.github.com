Final Project

Possible topic: Safe space for accepting all identities

Articles

  • "Self Identity Problems." Mental Health, Depression, Anxiety, Wellness, Family & Relationship Issues, Sexual Disorders & ADHD Medications, www.mentalhelp.net/understanding-your-problem/self-identity/.

    Talks about identity problems and crises. This article explains people’s identity and how people are not born with an identity but one develops it over time as they are growing up. The article also explains low self-esteem and low self-efficacy. The website from the article also gives other resources to help with mental health.

  • "10 Things to Remember When You’re Struggling & Feel Stuck." Tiny Buddha, 8 Feb. 2021, tinybuddha.com/blog/10-things-to-remember-when-youre-struggling-and-feel-stuck/.

    This article gives coping strategies and mechanisms for people struggling. It gives affirmative pointers that could possibly help someone who is struggling. Throughout the article it is connected to other articles on the same website about being “stronger than you think you are” and how to deal with the feeling of being judged.
